Details On Our Service

Our algorithm takes in details that you input to match you with shelters in Bergen County. We only ask about your sexuality, if you are bringing children, and if you have any physical or mental disabilities. (Some shelters do not let in people with physical/mental disabilities) We do not store or save any data.
