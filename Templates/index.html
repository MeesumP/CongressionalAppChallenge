About Us

Considering the close proximity of Bergen County and the 5th Congressional District of New Jersey to New York City, as well as the recent influx of people into the area, this service, Shelter Search, seeks to connect anyone suffering from homelessness to shelters that have programs meant to help them. This services is primarily intended for those with disabilities, children, or both. The end goal is that people reach the shleters organizations that are able to support them, eventually aiding both the shelters and those experiencing homelessness.

Details On Our Service

Our algorithm takes in details that you input to match you with shelters in Bergen County. We only ask about your gender, if you are bringing children, and if you have any physical or mental disabilities. (Some shelters do not let in people with physical/mental disabilities) We do not store or save any data.
